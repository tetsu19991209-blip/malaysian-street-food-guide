Jalan Alor Kuala Lumpur

Jalan Alor is arguably Kuala Lumpur's most famous food street. As the sun sets, the street transforms into a bustling open-air food court with a vibrant atmosphere. You can find everything from grilled chicken wings and satay to Hokkien mee, seafood, and a wide array of local fruits. It's a great spot for tourists looking to sample a wide variety of Malaysian street food in one place.

Petaling Street Kuala Lumpur

While known for its market stalls selling clothes and souvenirs, Petaling Street also has a long history as a food haven. Hidden within the bustling market, you'll find legendary stalls serving up traditional Chinese dishes like chee cheong fun, wantan mee, and claypot chicken rice. It's an authentic experience that takes you back in time.

New Lane Street Food Stalls, George Town, Penang

Penang is a legendary food destination, and New Lane is a must-visit. This food court-style street offers a fantastic variety of Penang's most celebrated dishes, including char koay teow, oyster omelette, and assam laksa. It's a popular spot for both locals and tourists seeking the authentic flavors of Penang.
