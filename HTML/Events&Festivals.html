Events and Festivals

Your guide to the best events and festivals that celebrate the foods that bring people together.

Upcoming Events

01

Ramadan Food Bazaar

March 14 to March 24, 2026

These iconic bazaars are a quintessential Malaysian food experience, appearing during the holy month of Ramadan. They are a treasure trove of traditional Malay street food and delicacies for breaking the fast.

02

Sabah International Food Expo

April 25 to April 27, 2026

This expo showcases a wide range of food and beverage products, bringing together local Sabah delights and other regional specialties. It's a great event for both food businesses and the public to explore the latest food trends.

03

Selangor Food Mega Festival

May 1 to May 4, 2026

A festival dedicated to two of Malaysia's most iconic culinary delights: satay and durian. The event celebrates the rich flavors of Selangor with a variety of satay recipes and an array of durian varieties.

04

Malaysian International Cocoa Fair

May 24 to May 27, 2026

This specialized event focuses on all things cocoa, from chocolate-making competitions and workshops to business matching sessions and product exhibitions

05

Havoc Food Festival

June 25 to June 29, 2026

Known for its vibrant atmosphere, this festival brings together a massive selection of popular and trending street food vendors, from viral stalls to classic local favorites

06

Food & Hospitality Malaysia

September 23 to September 26, 2025

This is a major trade-only event for the food and hospitality industry, but it also features exciting public-facing events like "Culinaire Malaysia," a prestigious culinary competition for top chefs

07

Malaysia International Gourmet Festival

15 October, 2025

22 October, 2025

29 October, 2025

A more high-end food event that showcases culinary brilliance through gourmet dining experiences, cooking demonstrations, and special-themed dinners by renowned chefs

08

Taste of Malaysia

31 August, 2025

This festival brings together a wide array of local street food and traditional dishes from various Malaysian states, offering a chance to taste the country's rich culinary diversity in one place.

09

Penang International Food Festival

31 August, 2025

This is a major event for foodies, showcasing Penang's famous street food scene and its rich culinary heritage. It features food carnivals, street food parties, cooking demonstrations, and special dining experiences.
