    🗣️ Local Phrases

    🍴 Eat-in or Takeaway
    BM ENG
    Bungkus Takeaway / To-go
    Makan sini Eat here / Dine-in
    Bawa balik Bring home / Takeaway
    Makan luar Eating outside (dining out)
    Tapau Casual Takeaway (slang, very common)

    💡 Tip: “Bungkus” and “Tapau” are the most common words locals use when ordering food to-go.
